# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 8, 2026

## Introduction

NWO Robotics ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our ChatGPT app.

## Information We Collect

### Through ChatGPT Integration
- **Conversation Data**: Commands and queries you send to control robots
- **Robot IDs**: Identifiers for robots you interact with
- **Task Data**: Task descriptions and execution results

### Through MCP Server
- **API Keys**: Authentication credentials (encrypted)
- **Command Logs**: Robot commands for debugging (anonymized)
- **Error Logs**: System errors for troubleshooting

## How We Use Your Information

- **Service Provision**: Execute robot commands and return results
- **Debugging**: Identify and fix issues with the service
- **Improvement**: Enhance robot control capabilities

## Data Storage

- Conversation data is processed in real-time and not stored
- Logs are retained for 30 days then automatically deleted
- No personal information is sold or shared with third parties

## Security

- All communications use TLS encryption
- API keys are encrypted at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only

## Your Rights

You can:
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of logging (may reduce service quality)
- Contact us with privacy concerns
